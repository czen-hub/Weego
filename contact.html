Driver Eligibility

What You'll Need to Join as a Driver.

WeGo follows California CPUC TNC requirements plus our own cooperative standards. If you're already driving for Uber or Lyft, you likely meet most of these already.

🪪

Identity & License

  • Valid California driver's license (or state license in your market)
  • Must be 21 years or older
  • At least 1 year of licensed driving experience
  • Social Security number for background check
🚗

Vehicle

  • 2010 or newer (4-door vehicle)
  • Valid California vehicle registration
  • Pass WeGo vehicle inspection (free at any Service Hub)
  • No commercial livery license required for standard tier
📋

Background Check

  • No major violations in past 7 years (DUI, reckless driving)
  • No felony convictions in past 7 years
  • Clean sex offender registry check
  • DMV driving record check (CPUC required)
💳

Membership Commitment

  • $500 founding fee payable over 12 months ($41.67/mo)
  • $75/month membership dues beginning with first payment
  • Agreement to cooperative bylaws and member code of conduct
  • Willingness to participate in at least one annual cooperative vote
💡
Already driving on Uber or Lyft?

You don't need to quit your current platform to join. We encourage dual-app driving during the pre-launch phase — your vesting clock starts from your first membership payment, not from your first WeGo ride.
